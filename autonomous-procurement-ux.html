Autonomous procurement UX

Non-human-present agentic commerce — surfaces automatically inside Claude / ChatGPT. Pick a scenario, then walk the six states.

A non-human-present shopping agent: it clarifies the request, sets a spending mandate, authorizes via an external Hedera browser flow that splits Intent and Payment mandates, runs sub-agents, raises an async approval-on-exception, and settles. Two scenarios: coffee (no age check) and alcohol (age verification up front).
